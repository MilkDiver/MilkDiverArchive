Game Treatment Proposal: Slime Mold

High Concept

Slime Mold is a simple visual simulation representing the movement of cells within a petri dish, with movement akin to slime molds.

Genre

Visual, Educational, Singleplayer, Motivational

Platform

Designed for Desktop Web, with potential for Mobile Web.

Story

An abstract simulation of a computer-generated slime mold. Slime mold is as slime mold does.

Aesthetics

The game will feature grid of tiles, which will be slowly covered by inactive green cells, and active blue cells. Aiming for a retro-lab-software aesthetic.

Gameplay

Players will be able to define initial conditions within the petri dish, as well as click to place new inactive cells to stimulate the blue cells to continue growing. There is no "learning" how to play, simply playing around and experimenting.
